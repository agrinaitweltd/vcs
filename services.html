Comprehensive Immigration & Visa Services

At Vantedge Immigration Consultants, we offer a full range of immigration and visa services tailored to meet your unique needs. Whether you're looking to study, work, invest, or reunite with family abroad, our expert team is here to guide you every step of the way.

Immigration Visa

Immigration visas are documents that allow individuals to enter a foreign country for the purpose of residing there permanently or for an extended period. Different countries have various types of immigration visas, each with specific eligibility requirements, application processes, and conditions.

02

Employment Visa

Employment-based visas are designed for individuals who have been offered employment opportunities in a foreign country. These visas allow foreign workers to legally work and reside in the host country for a specified duration.

LEARN MORE
03

Job Seeking Visa

Job seeker visas, also known as employment-seeking visas or work search visas, are a type of visa that allows individuals to enter a foreign country for the specific purpose of seeking employment opportunities.

04

Education Visa

Student visas are issued to international students who wish to pursue education in a foreign country. These visas allow students to enroll in full-time courses at accredited educational institutions.

05

Tourism Visa

Immigration tourism visas, also known as tourist visas or visitor visas, are a type of travel document issued by a country to individuals who wish to visit for leisure, recreation, or tourism purposes.
