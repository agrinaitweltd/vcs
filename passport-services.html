Comprehensive Passport Services

At Vantedge Immigration Consultants, we provide complete passport services including new applications, renewals, replacements for lost or damaged passports, and expedited processing when available. Our expert team ensures your passport application is handled professionally and efficiently.

Our Passport Services Include

New Passport Applications

Complete assistance with first-time passport applications, including documentation and submission.

Passport Renewals

Fast and efficient renewal services for expiring or expired passports.

Lost/Damaged Passports

Help with replacing lost, stolen, or damaged passports including police reports and affidavits.

Express Processing

Expedited services for urgent travel needs and emergency situations.

Required Documents

  • Completed passport application form
  • Birth certificate or national ID
  • Passport-sized photographs (recent)
  • Proof of citizenship
  • Payment receipt for passport fees
  • Old passport (for renewals)
  • Police report (for lost/stolen passports)

Processing Time

Standard Processing: 4-8 weeks from submission

Expedited Processing: 2-3 weeks (additional fees apply)

Emergency Processing: 1-5 business days (subject to availability and additional fees)
