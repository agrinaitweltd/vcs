Work and Study Permit Services

At Vantedge Immigration Consultants, we provide comprehensive assistance with work permits, student visas, and job-seeking visas. Whether you're pursuing career opportunities, education abroad, or exploring employment options, our expert team guides you through every step of the process.

Our Services Include

Employment Visas

Work permits and employment visas for professionals with job offers abroad. We handle all documentation and employer requirements.

Student Visas

Complete assistance for students pursuing education abroad, from application to enrollment and visa approval.

Job Seeking Visas

Guidance for professionals seeking employment opportunities in foreign countries with job search visa programs.

Post-Study Work Permits

Assistance with obtaining work permits after graduation to gain valuable international work experience.

Employment Visa Requirements

  • Valid job offer from a registered employer
  • Labor Market Impact Assessment (if required)
  • Educational credentials and certifications
  • Work experience documentation
  • Valid passport and photographs
  • Medical examination and police clearance
  • Proof of professional qualifications

Student Visa Requirements

  • Letter of acceptance from accredited institution
  • Proof of financial support for tuition and living expenses
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • English language proficiency test results (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Valid passport and photographs
  • Medical examination and health insurance
  • Statement of purpose
