Visa Applications

Expert Guidance for Immigration, Tourism & Visit Visas

Professional Visa Application Services

Immigration visas allow individuals to enter a foreign country for the purpose of residing there permanently or for an extended period. At Vantedge Immigration Consultants, we specialize in helping you navigate the complex visa application process with confidence.

Types of Visas We Process

Immigration Visas

Permanent residence visas for those looking to settle abroad. We assist with skilled migration, family sponsorship, and investor programs.

Tourist Visas

Short-term visitor visas for tourism, leisure, and family visits. We handle single-entry, multiple-entry, and transit visas.

Business Visas

For business meetings, conferences, and trade activities. We ensure all documentation meets embassy requirements.

Our Visa Application Process

1

Initial Consultation

We assess your eligibility and discuss visa options suitable for your circumstances.

2

Document Preparation

We provide a comprehensive checklist and help you gather all required documents.

3

Application Submission

We review, complete, and submit your application to the relevant authorities.

4

Follow-up & Updates

We track your application and keep you informed throughout the process.

Required Documents (General)

  • Valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
  • Completed visa application form
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of financial means
  • Travel itinerary and accommodation
  • Supporting documents (varies by visa type)
  • Visa application fee payment
